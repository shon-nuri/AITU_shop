1. What is AITU known for?

AITU (Astana IT University) is renowned for its focus on technological education, innovation, and preparing students for careers in IT and related fields. It emphasizes research, entrepreneurship, and collaboration with global tech leaders.

2. What kind of products are available in the web store?

The web store offers a variety of premium apparel, including hoodies, t-shirts, and other branded merchandise. Each product is crafted to combine style, quality, and comfort, reflecting AITU's innovative spirit.

3. How can I purchase items from the web store?

To purchase items, simply browse the store, add products to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can create an account for a smoother experience, but guest checkout is also available.
